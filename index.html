About Us

Born out of pure love for our own little Oreo, we created a space where your furry companions can feel at home, even when you’re away. Located in the heart of Newtown, Kolkata, our pet care center is designed to be warm, safe, and filled with tail wags and purrs. Whether it’s playtime, a cozy sleepover, or a relaxing grooming session, every pet receives personalized attention, affection, and care — just like they do at home. We welcome pets of all breeds, sizes and personalities and treat each one as uniquely as they are. Oreo’s House is more than a service — it’s a second home for your beloved pets, where their happiness comes first.
